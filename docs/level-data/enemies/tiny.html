Tiny

Small but deadly enemy with powerful attacks.

Stats​

  • Health: 50
  • Attack Damage: 50
  • XP Points: 300

Behavior​

  • Small but deadly
  • Powerful punch attack
  • Keeps its distance if you're looking at it

Strategy​

  • Sneak attacks work best
  • Use rings to get close undetected
  • Don't underestimate their damage
